Deacon St. John

My full name is Deacon Lee St. John, also known as Deek. Two years ago I used to be a soldier in the United States Army's 10th Mountain Division and an Enforcer of the Mongrels Motorcycle Club. Today I'm a survivor of the Freaker outbreak that has decimated the world across the Pacific Northwest. After losing my wife Sarah at the start of the outbreak, I started working as a ruthless mercenary accepting bounties from wildernesses encampments in exchange for supplies.
